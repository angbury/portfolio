Since 2016, I've gained vast experience in motion graphics and digital interactive projects for brands, government entities, and private companies. My expertise in digital design has allowed me to create and direct these projects successfully. My passion for animation and video games has been a driving force behind my continuous growth and learning in this field.

From 2019 to 2022, I served as a Career Academic in Digital Animation and Interactivity Engineering at UVM, where I had the opportunity to share my knowledge and experience with aspiring professionals. Currently, I am an instructor at UCamp, teaching Video Games and Augmented Reality. It brings me great joy to pass on my expertise and enthusiasm to my students.

Since May 2023, I have been working as a Technical Artist at Sticky Games, utilizing my extensive experience in motion graphics and game development for various brands.

While I have enjoyed teaching at the university, I am eager to return to the industry and embrace new challenges and opportunities in my two passions: Motion Design and Game Development. Additionally, I am interested in freelance writing and collaborating on different content writing project

With my extensive experience in digital design and my unwavering passion for these fields, I am confident that I will achieve further successes in the future.
